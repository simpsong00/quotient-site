[0 Comments](https://www.simpsong00.com/blog/viva-italia-photoblog#comments)

 

An old colleague from Italy messaged me last week that they were looking to buy my book when it came out, so this week, I thought I'd do a photoblog of a few locations in 
The Quantum Contingent 
that are in Italy, one of my favorite vacation spots! (If you ever go to Florence, please have some Gelato for me!) The book has a chapter in Florence, multiple chapters in Tuscany, and references Orvieto... Here are some of my photos of all the above! (
The Quantum Contingent
 specifically mentions the three things shown in the middle row of photos)

Divertitevi!

Greg
